Rustic Jute Rope Ottoman

Made by @PriyaDesigns • Uploaded 2 days ago
🪢
₹350 Total Cost
2 Hours Time to Make
Beginner Difficulty

Materials Needed

  • 1 Old discarded car tire (Got mine free from a local mechanic)
  • 20 meters of thick Jute rope
  • 1 Large bottle of industrial hot glue or Fevicol SR
  • 2 Round pieces of plywood (cut to the diameter of the tire)
  • Screws and a drill

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Prep the Base

Clean the tire thoroughly. Place one plywood circle on the top and one on the bottom. Secure them to the tire using your drill and screws so you have a solid flat surface to sit on.

Step 2: Start the Rope

Find the exact center of the top plywood circle. Apply a generous amount of hot glue and attach one end of the jute rope. Begin coiling the rope tightly in a spiral, gluing as you go.

Step 3: Wrap the Sides

Once the top is completely covered, continue wrapping the rope down the sides of the tire. Make sure there are no gaps between the layers of rope.

Step 4: Secure and Finish

When you reach the bottom edge, cut the rope and glue the final end securely tucked under the base. Let it dry for 24 hours before sitting on it!
